The type of events we make for you

general events

Those for the entire community department — students, researchers, professors and staff — irrespective of gender. Additionally, events that are open to the public, extending our reach to the broader external community associated with the department as well.

program meetings

We hold general meetings with everyone involved in the program — the Inspira team, mentors, and mentees. These meetings provide an opportunity to receive feedback on the program's progress, identify areas of success, challenges, and gather suggestions for improvements.

mentee's meetings

We also organize separated meetings, with the mentees, to understand how the program is progressing for them. These meetings are separate from those with mentors to ensure mentees feel comfortable expressing themselves and providing feedback.

mentor's meetings

Mentor meetings are also conducted separately from mentee meetings to ensure that mentors have the same opportunity as mentees to openly discuss the relationships they are forming and provide feedback without reservation.
